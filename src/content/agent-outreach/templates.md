## Usage Notes for Josh

- **Variant 1** is for cold outreach to agents you've researched and identified as targets. Personalize the opening if you have any mutual connection or specific knowledge about their roster.
- **Variant 2** is a single follow-up, sent 10-14 days after Variant 1. If no response after this, move to quarterly brief distribution (Variant 5 cadence) without further direct follow-up.
- **Variant 3** is triggered by engagement — LinkedIn article view, website visit, or any content interaction tracked in GHL.
- **Variant 4** is sent within 24 hours of a discovery call. The bracketed items MUST be filled with specific details from the conversation. This template is useless if it's generic.
- **Variant 5** is the long-game relationship builder. Sent quarterly to all agents who've had at least one meaningful interaction. The brief itself does the selling. The email just delivers it.
- **Never claim NHLPA endorsement or affiliation.**
- **Never guarantee performance or specific tax savings amounts in outreach emails.** The case study numbers in Variant 5 should reference anonymized, realistic scenarios.
