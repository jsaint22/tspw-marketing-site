# TSPW "Opening Faceoff" — Pre-Call Prep Email

**Status:** DRAFT — automated email template for GHL  
**Trigger:** Sent automatically after a prospect books a 30-minute consultation  
**Tone:** Personal. Warm. Direct. Should feel like Josh wrote it himself.

---

**Subject:** Looking forward to our call, [First Name]

---

[First Name],

Got your booking — looking forward to talking on [date] at [time].

Here's what the 30 minutes looks like:

**First 10 minutes — I listen.** I want to understand where you are right now. Where you are in your career, what's on your mind financially, what's keeping you up at night. No forms to fill out. No questionnaire. Just a conversation.

**Next 10 minutes — I share what I see.** Based on what you tell me, I'll give you an honest assessment of where I think the biggest financial risks and opportunities are in your situation. I'll be direct. If I see something that needs attention, I'll say it.

**Last 10 minutes — we figure out if this makes sense.** If Top Shelf Private Wealth is the right fit for your situation, I'll explain exactly how it works — the flat fee, the planning process, what you'd get. If it's not the right fit, I'll tell you that too and point you in the right direction.

A couple things that might be useful to think about before we talk:

- What's the thing about your financial situation that bugs you the most?
- Do you currently work with a financial advisor? If so, what's working and what isn't?
- Is there a specific financial question you've been wanting an honest answer to?

You don't need to prepare anything. You don't need to bring documents. Just show up and be straight with me. I'll do the same.

One more thing — everything we discuss is confidential. I'm a fiduciary. That means I'm legally obligated to act in your best interest, and your privacy is non-negotiable.

Talk soon,

Josh St. Laurent, CFP, CFT  
Top Shelf Private Wealth  
topshelfprivatewealth.com

P.S. — If something comes up and you need to reschedule, no stress. Just use the link in your confirmation email. I'd rather talk when you're fully present than squeeze in a call that doesn't feel right.

---

## Template Variables (for GHL automation)

| Variable | Source |
|----------|--------|
| `[First Name]` | Contact first name |
| `[date]` | Appointment date, formatted as "Tuesday, May 6" |
| `[time]` | Appointment time with timezone, formatted as "2:00 PM PT" |

## Notes for Josh

- This email should go out within 5 minutes of booking confirmation.
- The P.S. is intentional — it lowers pressure and demonstrates respect for their time. Keep it.
- If the prospect mentioned anything specific in their intake form (e.g., "just got traded," "signing my first contract," "worried about taxes"), a future version of this template should dynamically reference that. For now, the generic version works.
- Do NOT include any marketing materials, PDFs, or attachments. The email is the experience. Keep it clean.
